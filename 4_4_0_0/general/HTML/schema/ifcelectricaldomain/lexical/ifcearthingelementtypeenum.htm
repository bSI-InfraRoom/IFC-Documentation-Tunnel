ConstantDescription
EARTHINGSTRIP

Definition according to EN 62561-2: for use in earth-termination and lightning protection systems as well as for ring equipotential bonding.

GROUNDINGPLATE

Definition according to IEC 62561-2: Earth electrode consisting of a metal plate buried in the ground.

GROUNDINGROD

Definition according to IEC 62561-2: Earth electrode consisting of a metal rod driven into the ground.

FIXEDTERMINAL

Definition according to EN 62561-2: for installation in concrete as a corrosion-free connection to the earth-termination system for protective equipotential bonding and / or functional equipotential bonding of the down conductor

GROUNDINGMESH

Definition according to IEC 60050-531: An electrode in the form of a mesh, and designed to establish an equipotential plan.

NOTDEFINED

Undefined type.

USERDEFINED

User-defined type.
