Semantic definitions at the type
Type definition

This enumeration defines the different method predefined types for face excavation that can specify an IfcFaceExcavation.

Enumeration definition
ConstantDescription
CROSSCONNECTION

Excavation linking two existing tunnels or openings.

FULLFACE

Excavation method where the entire tunnel face is worked simultaneously.

PILOTBORE

Initial excavation along the axis of the tunnel with a smaller cross-section than the final tunnel, which is later enlarged to the full cross-section.

REAMING

Enlargement of an existing tunnel opening through face excavation.

SHAFT

Excavation method used for vertical or inclined openings, such as access shafts.

STAGED

Progressive excavation of parts of the tunnel face in sequential steps or stages.

USERDEFINED

User-defined type.

NOTDEFINED

Undefined type.
