Properties for the results of in-situ pumping tests in soil or rock.

Properties

PSD-XML

NameTypeDescription
HydraulicPermeability P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLinearVelocityMeasure A measurement of the ability of a fluid to flow through a rock. Represented by the variable "k". The quality of the soil that enables water to move through the profile. Permeability is measured as the number of metres per sec that water moves through the saturated soil.
HydraulicConductivity P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLinearVelocityMeasure Hydraulic Conductivity (permeability) of soil for water, given with the K or Kf value in m/s
Transmissivity P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcReal Rate of flow of groundwater under a unit hydraulic gradient through an aquifer of unit width and unit thickness. That is, transmissivity is the product of hydraulic conductivity and thickness of the aquifer. The unit is in m2/second.
Storativity P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveRatioMeasure Volume of water released from storage per unit surface area of a confined aquifer per unit decline in hydraulic head. It is dimensionless.
LugeonValue P_LISTVALUE / IfcLabel Lugeon Value = (q / L) x (P0 / P), where:
  • q: flow rate [lit/min]
  • L: Length of the borehole test interval [m]
  • P0: reference pressure of 1 MPa [MPa]
  • P: Test pressure [MPa]
